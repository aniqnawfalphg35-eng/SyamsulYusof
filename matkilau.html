The film is inspired by the epic Malaysian historical figure Mat Kilau who had fought against the British colonists in Pahang before independence.

Mat Kilau: Kebangkitan Pahlawan is the first film produced by Studio Kembara.

It is the 11th film directed by Syamsul Yusof and is the first film he has directed without acting in and also the first film he has directed under another production company, not his family's Skop Productions.

In August 2017, he announced his intention to direct an epic film about the history of the nation's heroes.

Filming was carried out on 1 April 2018 with a Production Fund grant of RM1.5 million and a Marketing Fund of RM300,000 allocated by FINAS, while the remaining balance was from Studio Kembara.

The film received an early warm reception in Malaysia with the highest opening day collection of RM1 million for a local film in two years, becoming the first epic genre film to break even upon release. Mat Kilau continued to explode when it managed to collect RM7 million in three days of screening in cinemas, based on the latest information obtained by the producer.
