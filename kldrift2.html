Evolusi KL Drift 2 is the third film directed by Syamsul Yusof and co-produced by Skop Productions and Grand Brilliance.

The film's production cost is estimated at RM 2.5 million, including promotion, making it the most expensive film ever produced by Skop.

The film's filming locations include Port Klang and Putrajaya.

The Evolution of KL Drift 2 was released on 25 March 2010 and managed to achieve the highest box office collection in all Malaysian cinemas for its first weekend of release (25 to 28 March), surpassing Hollywood blockbusters such as How to Train Your Dragon and Alice in Wonderland.

It was reported that in its first week in cinemas, the film's grosses reached RM 3.6 million in 70 cinemas nationwide, including RM560,000 on the first day and RM900,000 on the third day.

At the 13th day mark (April 6), the total grosses increased further to RM 5.2 million.
