KL Gangster 2 tells the story of how two brothers, Malek and Jai, were indirectly caught up in the gangster world after their father's death 10 years ago.

Since their father's death, their family's life has been in turmoil alongside their mother.

The film, which began filming on 1 December 2011, was shot in several interesting locations such as around Kuala Lumpur, the Klang Valley and Batu Gajah, Perak, and took 45 days to complete.

The script was co-written by Syamsul Yusof and Rizal Ashreff. The cost of making the film was estimated at RM 4.51 million, including promotion.

The film was released on 3 October 2013 in cinemas across Malaysia. KL Gangster 2 managed to collect a high amount of RM3 million in its five-day screening, starting on 3 October, including three days of early screening from 27 to 29 September.

On 28 August 2013, the whole of Malaysia was shocked by the illegal distribution of this film via the internet. The film's production company,

Skop Productions Sdn Bhd, took immediate action by filing a report with the authorities to curb the widespread distribution of this film. Its director, Syamsul Yusof, has issued a stern warning to those responsible for doing so.
