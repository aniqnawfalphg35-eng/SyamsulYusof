n 2007, Syamsul Yusof directs his first feature film, EVOLUSI KL DRIFT co-written by him and his cousin Rizal Ashreff was initially rejected by his father for further redrafting. After the revision, his first film project was finally approved alongside a budget of RM 1.35 million.

Produced by Skop Productions, the film relied on heavily modified vehicles and real professional drift stunt drivers to capture accurate mechanical physics during high-speed chases across city streets.

Despite his lack of directing experience and the immense pressure of keeping reputation of his family, Evolusi KL Drift was well-received upon its release. The high-octane racing film officially hit cinemas on 3 April 2008. It captivated target audiences nationwide, generating an instant resurgence in the local automotive action movie genre.

The film achieved substantial financial triumph, finalizing its domestic theatrical run with a strong box office haul of RM4.5 million, proving to be a highly lucrative payout against its focused production budget.
